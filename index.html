We are a family office. That means we use a fully integrated, independent, multidisciplinary approach to wealth management that helps you and your family understand, build and sustain your wealth.

Family focused and privately owned for 25 years, our firm serves clients nationwide from our offices in Gurgaon and St. Louis. Our associates combine investment management, wealth planning and coordination, and culture and legacy services to build solutions reflecting the needs, perspectives and objectives of each family.

By transferring both wealth and wisdom through generations, we empower your family to make intelligent choices that inform and define your vision, ultimately delivering your family’s fullest potential.
